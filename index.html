Jyothi Swaroopa Jinka

Email/ Google Scholar/ GitHub/ LinkedIn/ CV

I am an MS Research student at CVIT, IIIT Hyderabad, where I work on computer vision and document AI. My research focuses on grounded document question answering, multilingual vision-language models, and scalable training for large document intelligence systems.

My work on multilingual textual attribute recognition (TexTAR) received the Best Student Paper Award at ICDAR 2025. I am also part of the BharatGen initiative under India's National AI Mission, where I co-developed Patram-7B-Instruct — a multilingual vision-language model for document understanding across Indian languages.

Broadly, my research touches on three themes: evidence-grounded reasoning in documents, multilingual and multi-domain document understanding, and scalable training infrastructure for vision-language models.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Information Technology from CBIT, Hyderabad.
